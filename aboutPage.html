Mood & Weather Analysis with Music

How does the weather affect your music listening habits?


a brief explanation


    This is an interactive web application that allows you to input a location and your Last.fm username, then generates a page showing your most-listened-to artists depending on the weather. The goal of this project is to provide a tool for self-reflection. The idea is to gain some insight into the way your mood is affected by the weather, and how that plays into your music listening habits. I hope this answers a curiosity about your own unconscious habits!
